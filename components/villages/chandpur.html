Chandpur

Vikasnagar block, Dehradun (Uttrakhand)

Chandpur Kala and Horawala both villages are situated in Vikasnagar Tehshil in Derhadun district of Uttarakhand State, India.

Chandpur village having population of about 700 (318 eligible for voting) situated near the bank of the Kali river. It is the 17 th smallest village by area in the sub district having geographical area of 61.98 hectares. Location code of the village is 045014

Mr Sanju is the Pradhan of Chandpur village and Mr Ravindra is the Pradhan of Horawala village.

Agriculture is the main source of income of the Chandpur village. Wheat, Maize, Rice, Mustards and cash crops like vegetables, ginger, mango grows in the Chandpur village. Type of soil is Mold (Domat) soil.

There is 1 Government Primary School, 1 Government Inter college and two private schools named (1) Anunaad Public School (2) EcoleGlobale Girls International School in the Chandpur- Horawala cluster. Students are aware of different career choices and mostly of them are interested in choosing Army, Police ,Teacher, Doctor as their career options. The overall literacy rate of Horawala village is 79.72%, with male literacy rate being 88.31% and female being 71.08%.

20% of the population of the Chandpur cluster is categorized as OBC/SC.

Village Chandpur lags in the healthcare facilities. There is no hospital and no Primary Healthcare Center (PHC) in the village.
