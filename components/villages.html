MEERPUR

The village Meerpur-Muwazarpur is located in Bahadarabad block of Haridwar district in Uttarakhand. It is at a distance of 17 km from Roorkee and about 5 km apart from NH-58 which connects Roorkee to Haridwar. The nearest major Railway Stations are Roorkee and Haridwar Junction.

CHANDPUR

The Chandpur Horawala includes Chanpur Khurd, Chandpur Kala and Horawala. This cluster is located at the banks of Kali River in Vikasnagar block of Dehradun district in Uttarakhand. Chandpur Kala and Chandpur Khurd come under the Gram Panchayat-Chandpur Khurd whereas Horawala is a separate Gram Panchayat. This cluster is approximately 110 kms away from Roorkee.

CHHARBA

Chharba is a large village located in Sahaspur block of Dehradun district, Uttarakhand, with total 1425 families residing. Chharba covers an area of about 1567.9 hectares. It is situated 7 km away from sub-district headquarter, Vikasnagar, and 22 km away from district headquarter Dehradun.

BELADI

The village Beladi-Salhapur is located in Roorkee block of Haridwar district in Uttarakhand. It is approximately 7 km away from Roorkee. The nearest major railway stations are Haridwar and Roorkee Junction. It was identified and adopted under UBA on February 7, 2015.

PURANPUR

The village Puranpur-Salhapur is located in Bahadrabad block of Haridwar district in Uttarakhand. It is at a distance of 29 km from Roorkee. The nearest major Railway Stations are Roorkee and Haridwar Junction. The Puranpur village comes under Meerpur cluster
