  • Sensing the need to make a child aware of physical abuse at an early stage rather than waiting for time to preach, team UBA Meerpur organized a Sexual Harassment Awareness Camp for girls of classes 8th, 9th and 10th. Ms Mrunali Damania, a PhD student at IIT Roorkee delivered a session on ‘How to be aware of the presence of evils around you’.
  • The camp was conducted successfully on October 13, 2018 with an overwhelming participation of more than 100 girl students of class 8th, 9th and 10th of Junior High School, Meerpur.
    Girls got to know about a new aspect of life and were highly motivated to act in the correct direction if any kind of incident happens with them in future.
  • The guest speaker Ms Mrunali Damania was very happy to see such an overwhelming response.
    Further initiatives relating to girl safety and hygiene have also been conducted in different schools in the village of Meerpur.
