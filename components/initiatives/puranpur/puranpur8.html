Republic Day Events
  • On 26th January 2019, Unnat Bharat Abhiyan IIT Roorkee organized a Republic Day fun event in a school of village, Puranpur. The team organized a drawing competition, a quizzing event, and fun races for primary students after their Republic Day parade.
  • What was more exciting was the prizes. The children were given shirts that were painted in an event ‘Rang-de-Zindagi’ organized by UBA IITR in Thomso, IIT Roorkee along with chocolates and crayon pencils. Our subtle efforts would go a long way to bring smiles to the faces of these children.

    It is with this social encouragement, we seek to build the future, of India.
