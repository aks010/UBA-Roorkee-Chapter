School Adornment
A wall painting event was organized with the help of members of Fine Arts Section, IIT Roorkee in Primary School II.Two paintings were made with a motive to encourage students to beautify their school. In the first phase of this initiative, the team with the help of members of Fine Arts Section, IIT Roorkee organized a wall painting event in Primary School 2 on August 18, 2018.
In the second phase, realizing the need of dustbins in the classes the team conducted a best out of waste session on September 8, 2018 in Primary School 2 of Chharba in which students were encouraged to create dustbins out of waste material. A similar session of best out of waste was conducted in Madrasa of Chharba on February 9, 2018 in which various activities to utilize waste materials like plantation using plastic bottles, making pencil holder using newspaper and a pencil box using bottles were organized.
