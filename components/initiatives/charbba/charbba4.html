Agricultural Survey
  • With the significant developments in the field of agriculture, new farming practices and techniques are prevailing, which are more profitable. So to give an insight to the villagers on adopting such practices, a team of 15 members conducted a survey on October 6, 2018 in the wards 1, 2, 3, 11, 14, 15 of village Chharba. The details obtained from the survey can help the team in taking up new initiatives on spreading awareness about these practices inthe village. The new practices included farming of lemon grass, mushroom and aromatic plants which make good profit. A total of 58 families of wards 1, 2, 3, 11, 14, 15 were surveyed during the survey.
    • The survey included questions related to the following topics:
    • Main Profession of the family
    • Area of land available for farming
    • Organic Farming
    • Aromatic Plants Production
    • Poly House
    • Biogas Plant
    • Mushroom Production
    • Cattle
    • Women Employment
