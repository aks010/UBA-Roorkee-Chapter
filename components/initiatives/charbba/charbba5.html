Cycle Donation
  • As Chharba occupies quite a large area, there are many students who belong to the distant areas of village and have to walk through a long distance to reach GIC (Government Inter College), Chharba. This leads to physical exhaustion and there are instances where children are unwilling to go to school because of the long distance and their incapability to afford a bicycle. So team UBA targeted various bhawans in IIT Roorkee in order to procure unclaimed cycles for the donation purpose
  • The cycle donation camp was organized on February 02, 2019 in GIC, Chharba. Team contacted the parents of all the recipients and invited them for the event. The team also invited Mrs Seema Devi, thepradhan of Chharba as a chief guest for the event.
  • The donation of cycles to the students with good academic records motivated even other students to work harder in their studies and get awarded in future.
