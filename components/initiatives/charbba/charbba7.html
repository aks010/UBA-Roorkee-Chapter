Best Out of Waste
A best out of waste session was conducted on September 08, 2018 in which students were encouraged to create dustbins. Necessary material was provided by the team and a sample was prepared in front of them. The active participation and good creative skills of students in the best out of waste session motivated the team to conduct more sessions of this kind. In the best out of waste session, the team members conducted various activities to utilize waste materials which included plantation using plastic bottles, making a pencil holder using newspaper and a pencil box using bottles. While in the ICT session team members imparted the basic information of Ms paint and word to the students by conducting a practical demonstration.
By taking such small steps team UBA aims to bring a positive change for the society.
