Information Communication Technology
  • Considering the technical advancements going on in modern world, team decided to introduce the students of primary schools of Chharba with basics of computers. Therefore, an informative session was conducted on basics of Microsoft Office under the initiative Information and Communication Technology (ICT).

    The first session was conducted on November 03, 2018 in the Primary School I and II in which the students were taught basics of MS Paint, MS Word and MS PowerPoint. A total of 50 students attended the session.
  • In the next phase of the initiative, the team conducted ICT session on MS Paint and MS Word in Madrasa of Chharba on February 09, 2019. 45 students attended the session and they were also provided with handouts of each topic which was being demonstrated in the class.
  • To make the dreams of almost thousand of students true, the team of Unnat Bharat Abhiyan, IIT ROORKEE and the management of Madarsa are working hand in hand. On one side where the staff of Madarsa is providing quality education to almost 850 students with 500 girls, on the other side Unnat Bharat Abhiyan, IIT Roorkee is opening new doors for them to explore.
  • On Saturday, the team organized ICT (Information and Communication Technology) sessions in the Madarsa of the village, Chharba to meet the objective of providing opportunities to deprived rural children.
