Science Exhibition
  • With an aim of nurturing the interest of the students towards science, science exhibition was conducted in the Government Secondary School Beladi on October 30, 2018 to give an insight to the students on the practical use of science to develop technologies that can be used for the benefit of the society. The team taught the students the working principle behind Balloon Boat and also helped them built one .The very purpose of the science exhibition was fulfilled and was marked by the active involvement of the students.
