Cycle Donation

To increase the interest of student towards academics, the team decided to award the academically bright students of secondary and primary school with unclaimed bicycles of the institute on February 16 2019. The names of the academically bright students of class 9th and 10th were given by the principal of the secondary school and the team successfully organized the camp. With due permission of Department of Student Welfare, IIT Roorkee, team UBA took hold of 7 unclaimed bicycles from the institute, repaired them and donated them to the secondary school students. The smile on the face of students spoke about the success of the initiative.
