Self Defense Camp
  • Self-defence especially for women is of utmost importance in the kind of world we live in today. In a country like India where the cases of gender violence are on rise, out of which many go unreported, self-defence has become a necessity more than ever. The vision behind organizing the camp was to give the girls of the village the ability to protect and overcome an attacker. Knowing that if one has the ability to defend themselves gives them the confidence and freedom to fully explore the world, meet new people and find new ways to engage with others.
  • Seeking to empower village school girls in personal safety and train them to escape from violence and crime unleashed against them, UBA, IIT Roorkee in collaboration with Taekwondo group, IIT Roorkee decided to organize a self-defence camp for the girls of the village Meerpur.
